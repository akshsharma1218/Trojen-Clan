F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can we contact the seller,renter?

    You can contact the seller through his/her email id which is displayed in this portal.

  • What if we lost the rented book?

    If you lost any book which you borrowed on a rental basis , then you have to pay us some designated amount which is close to the MRP of the book.

  • How many books can we sell?

    There are no set limit to the quantity of books that one can sell or buy.

  • Can we rent multiple books?

    You can rent as many academic books as you want. But in case of journals and novels , you can rent them for one month at max. After that period you need to reorder it again.

  • What types of books can be sold or rented?

    In case of academic books the seller holds the monopoly over the mode he/she prefers to barter the book, either selling or renting.But novels and journals can only be rented and not sold.

  • Can i rent a book for an year?

    Yes, you can rent an academic book for one year but again this is not applicable to novels and journals which can be rented for one month at max.
